All of the prompts in this experiment will be objects. In each case, you'll be asked to think of as many uses for each of these objects as you can, and to type them in, one by one, in a response field.

No matter what, it is important that you type in every idea that pops into you your head as soon as it does. It is not necessary that your idea "make sense", but try and describe your idea exactly as it came to you.

When you are ready to begin, press "Begin Practice." You will then be given further instructions about a practice trial.
