Task Instructions

This is a practice trial. It will last 30 seconds. It is designed to help you get used to the procedure for the experiment.

When you press "Start", the name of a specific category will appear on the screen.

As soon as you think of an example of that category, type it into the field and press ENTER.

It is important to keep thinking of responses and to type them in for the entire time

Please type them in one at a time as they come to you, and press ENTER after typing each one.

Pressing ENTER will clear the response field so that you can continue to type new responses.

When the 30 seconds are over, you will recieve instructions about the first real experimental trial.
